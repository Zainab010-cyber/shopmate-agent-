# Return Policy

Campus Shop accepts returns within **30 days** of delivery for unused items in original packaging.

**Eligible for return:** Unopened electronics, accessories, and apparel in resaleable condition.
**Not eligible:** Opened software licenses, personalised items, and clearance sale items marked "final sale."

To start a return:
1. Contact support with your order ID.
2. Receive a return authorisation (RA) number.
3. Ship the item back within 7 days of RA issuance.

Return shipping is free for defective or incorrect items. For change-of-mind returns, a AED 15 restocking fee may apply.

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item.
