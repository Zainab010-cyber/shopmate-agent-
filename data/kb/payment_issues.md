# Payment Issues

**Double charge:** If you see two charges for the same order, contact support immediately with your order ID and bank statement details. Double-charge cases are escalated to our billing team within 24 hours.

**Failed payment but order confirmed:** This is a rare sync error. Do not reorder — contact support with your order ID.

**Declined card:** Verify billing address matches your bank records. Campus Shop accepts Visa, Mastercard, and Apple Pay.

**Installment plans:** Available for orders over AED 1,500 through select UAE banks. Installment queries are handled by our billing team via escalation.

The support agent cannot access full card numbers and will never ask for CVV or PIN.
