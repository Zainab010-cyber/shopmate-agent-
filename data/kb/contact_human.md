# Speaking to a Human Agent

You can request a human agent at any time during your support conversation.

**Human agents are available:** Sunday–Thursday, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM GST.

**Cases automatically escalated to humans:**
- Payment disputes and double charges
- Damaged or defective item claims
- Refund requests over AED 2,000
- Account security concerns

When escalated, you will receive a ticket reference number and a human agent will respond within 4 business hours.
