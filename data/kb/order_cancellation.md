# Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled free of charge if status is **processing** and has not yet shipped.

**Cannot cancel:** Orders with status "shipped" or "delivered" — use the return policy instead.

To cancel, provide your order ID. Cancellations are processed immediately and refunds follow the standard refund timeline (5-7 business days).

If your order shows "processing" for more than 3 business days, contact support for a status update.
