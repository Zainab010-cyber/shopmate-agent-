# Student Discount Programme

Campus Shop offers **10% off** for verified university students in the UAE.

**Eligibility:** Active students with a valid .ac.ae or partner university email address.

**How to verify:** Register on Campus Shop, go to Account → Student Verification, and confirm your university email. Verification completes within 24 hours.

**Exclusions:** Discount does not apply to clearance items, gift cards, or extended warranty add-ons.

Discount status can be checked in Account Settings. The support agent cannot manually apply or verify student discounts.
