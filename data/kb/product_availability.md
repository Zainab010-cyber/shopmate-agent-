# Product Availability

Campus Shop restocks popular items weekly. Out-of-stock items show an estimated restock date on the product page.

**Back-in-stock notifications:** Sign up on the product page with your email.

**Pre-orders:** Available for select upcoming laptop models. Pre-orders ship on the announced release date; cancellation allowed until 48 hours before ship.

The support agent cannot reserve inventory or guarantee restock dates. For bulk orders (10+ units), request a quote via escalation.
