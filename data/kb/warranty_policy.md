# Warranty Policy

All electronics sold by Campus Shop include a **1-year manufacturer warranty** covering hardware defects.

**Covered:** Manufacturing defects, battery failure within 6 months, dead-on-arrival units.
**Not covered:** Accidental damage, water damage, unauthorised repairs, normal wear and tear.

Warranty claims require proof of purchase (order ID). Extended warranty (2-year) is available at checkout for laptops and tablets.

For warranty service, contact support to receive an RMA number and shipping instructions to our authorised service centre.
