# Refund Policy

Refunds are issued to the original payment method only.

**Processing times:**
- Credit/debit card: 5-7 business days after approval
- Campus Shop gift cards: immediate store credit
- Bank transfer: 7-10 business days

Refunds require manager approval for orders over AED 2,000. The support agent can initiate an escalation but cannot authorise refunds directly.

Partial refunds may be issued for returned items with missing accessories. Full refunds apply to cancelled orders before shipment and for verified damaged or defective items.
