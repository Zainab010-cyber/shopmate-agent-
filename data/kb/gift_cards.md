# Gift Cards

Campus Shop gift cards are available in denominations of AED 50, 100, 250, and 500.

**Redemption:** Enter the gift card code at checkout. Multiple gift cards can be combined on one order.

**Balance check:** Log in and go to Account → Gift Cards, or contact support with the last 4 characters of your gift card code.

**Expiry:** Gift cards are valid for 2 years from purchase date. Non-refundable once purchased.

Gift card balance issues are escalated to the billing team.
