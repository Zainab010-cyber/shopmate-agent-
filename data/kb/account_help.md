# Account Help

**Password reset:** Use "Forgot Password" on the Campus Shop login page. Reset links expire in 1 hour.

**Update email address:** Log in → Account Settings → Profile. Email changes require verification.

**Order history:** All past orders appear under Account → My Orders. Guest checkout orders can be looked up by order ID and email.

**Student discount:** Verify your .ac.ae student email to receive 10% off. Discount applies at checkout after verification.

The support agent can look up orders by order ID but cannot change account passwords or email addresses directly.
