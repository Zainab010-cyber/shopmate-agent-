# Damaged or Defective Items Policy

If your item arrives damaged or is defective, you are entitled to a **full refund or replacement** within **7 days** of delivery.

**Steps to report damaged goods:**
1. Contact support within 7 days with your order ID.
2. Provide a brief description of the damage.
3. Photos may be requested (optional for agent-assisted cases).

We will create a priority escalation for our fulfillment team. You do not need to return damaged electronics before receiving a replacement, but you may be asked to return the damaged unit once the replacement ships.

Damaged-item cases are escalated automatically to a human agent for verification and refund authorisation.
