# Shipping Policy

Campus Shop offers standard and express shipping within the UAE.

**Standard shipping:** 3-5 business days. Free on orders over AED 200.
**Express shipping:** 1-2 business days. AED 25 flat rate.

Orders placed before 2:00 PM GST ship the same business day. International shipping is not currently available.

You can track your order using the tracking number provided in your confirmation email or by providing your order ID to our support agent.

If your package is delayed beyond the estimated delivery window, contact support with your order ID for investigation.
